Disclaimer

This tool is to approximate the year of a Squier guitar. This tool in no way validates that a guitar is in fact a Squier. Also due to the use of third party data some dates might be missing. For instance as of 2017.09.27 most serial numbers past 2011 are not included. I am working on adding the most recent years.

References

Primary Resource:
Squier Electrics 30 years of Fender's budget guitar brand, by Tony Bacon
Additional Resource:
guitarrepairbench.com's Guitar Dating: Korean Fender Serial Numbers was used to approximate date cut offs when Squier / Fender switched countries or used different prefixes.
